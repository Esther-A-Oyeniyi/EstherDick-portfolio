Sales Data Project

This project analyse the daily sales, products and promotion strategy engaged by the brewery company. I sought to find the relationship between the products available and marketing strategy by comparing the sales volume of each account type. I also explored the data to identify the best/worst performing accounts and what factors may have contributed to the outcome. This analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel with specific features like Macros for data cleaning and other data analytical expressions.

AI in Construction

Construction is being revolutionized by artificial intelligence (AI), which uses data analysis to optimize scheduling, project management, and resource allocation. Drones powered by artificial intelligence (AI) deliver real-time footage for monitoring and inspections, while machine learning forecasts hazards to improve site safety. This project was performed as a bibliometric analysis. It was conducted to examine the extent of knowledge and application of AI in construction.
